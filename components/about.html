About TazonChemist

Your trusted healthcare partner for over 28 years, serving our community with dedication and expertise.

Our Story

Founded in 1996 by Enock, TazonChemist began as a small neighborhood pharmacy with a simple mission: to provide personalized healthcare services that put patients first.

What started as a single location has grown into a trusted healthcare hub, but our commitment to personal care remains unchanged. We believe that every patient deserves individual attention, expert guidance, and genuine care.
